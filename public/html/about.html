The imagine clouds

ImagineClouds was founded in August 2020, in Minas Gerais Brazil. It was created and developed by a group of young people, who saw an opportunity, in a cloud themed company. Our group is graduating in the area of Technology, so we chose to invest in a company that served our area and allowed us to expand our knowledge. The idea for the cloud theme came from a childhood memory, from when we layed down to look at the shapes of the clouds.
